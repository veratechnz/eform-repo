Configure the site timeout. The higher the timeout setting, the longer a page can be idle before being logged out.

Set which enabled company managers are set as manager on your draft and completed forms. 


Configure how long after creation your form will be archived. Once archived the form can only be retrieved as a PDF and not editable again. 


Choose which font you want you content to use when populated on your forms. 


Set all your submitted content to bold on all your eForms.

Setting this to enabled prevents any salespeople from editing any of your company created clauses on a form.

Enabling this setting will turn off the use of REINZ clauses on all forms for all your staff.
